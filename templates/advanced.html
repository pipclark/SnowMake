SNOWMAKES

ADVANCED

More Control, less random, more fun?


Play with the starting height of your snowflake in the cloud (which affects temperature) and the humidity (water vapor supersaturation) in the cloud, and see how this affects the snowmakes result. Note that the higher up in the cloud your snowflake starts it's journey, the longer it will take to fall to you (/load). The bottom of the cloud is set to 2000m, below this the relative humidity will fall below 100% and the snowflake will stop growing.
You can also turn on the options for branches overlapping being allowed, and size changes on (some snowflakes will be smaller than others, how do start height and humidity affect their size?)
Check out the graphs of temperature and humidity for the conditions you set below, and take a look at how it works for more background.
