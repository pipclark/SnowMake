SNOWMAKES

The random snowflake generator

Thanks for visiting my snowflake generating website! My name's Pip and I'm the cohost and editor for the Real Science Questions Podcast. I started the podcast in summer 2021 with my friend Tara so that we could explore scientific topics that interest us, and attempt to answer our scientific questions! While researching why snowflakes are unique and how they form for our Christmas bonus episode in 2021 I felt inspired to try and make a simple 2D unique snowflake generator - snowmakes!

- check out the how it works section if you want more technical details, and please check out the real science questions podcast Christmas episode for the snow discussion, plus some lighter parts about if you could ever safely eat yellow snow, how reindeers would have to change to be able to fly, and how rudolph could really have a brightly growing nose! It's available on spotify, google podcasts, iTunes, pocket casts, audible, amazon music, and so on! Or listen on our website (link below).
If you have any questions / comments send me an email: rsqpodcast@gmail.com

Thanks for visiting! - Pip
